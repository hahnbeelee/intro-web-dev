"The best reason to paint is that there is no reason to paint."

-Keith Haring

Hello, I'm Rebecca! Drawing, creating, and designing has always been a passion of mine, and I'm glad that I can be expressive with these things through my artwork. I have a vast range of art-- from transforming ordinary words into characteristic fonts, to painting sceneries around quotes on canvas, I love producing interesting and unique designs for my clients.

Outside of my designing, I'm a 3rd year student at Cornell University, studying Electrical & Computer Engineering. I also am the social and events chair for my sorority, Phi Mu, and am an avid dancer in Rise Dance Group at Cornell.
